Thank you for your order request!


The doctor will review your request, contact you with any questions, and notify you of denial or approval via your preferred contact method.


Upon approval you will be emailed an invoice which you can easily pay by clicking on the link in your email.

You do not need to have a PayPal account in order to pay, just scroll to the bottom of the login screen to enter your credit card information.

Once paid, the medication will be shipped to the address you have provided.
